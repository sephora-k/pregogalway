About Us

   

Prego Shoe Boutique opened it's doors on the October 31st 1997.
It is located in High Street, Galway. Providing top quality women's shoes, boots and handbags at affordable prices.
The proud owner of Prego, Lucy Simpson, moved from Offaly to Galway to open her own shoe boutique and has been successful ever since. With its wide range of footwear from exclusive Italian brands to handbangs from Spain, Holland and Ireland.
